sorting algorithm let $a=(a_1,\dots,a_n)$ be a given array.
first we divide it into increasing runs $a=(r_1,\dots,r_k)$.
for example, if $a=(1,3,7,6,5,2,4)$ then the runs are $(1,3,7),(6),(5),(2,4)$.
then we place the runs into a min-heap, ordered by length.
now, as long as there is more than one run, we remove the two shortest runs and insert their merged run.
